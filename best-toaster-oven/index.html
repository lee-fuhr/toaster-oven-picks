Quick answer

The Cuisinart TOA-60 is our top pick for most people, balancing air frying, toasting, and baking without breaking the bank.

The Breville BOV845BSS is for those who want premium performance and precision, while the Hamilton Beach HTO100 is a steal for budget shoppers who need basic toasting and small baking.

Toaster ovens have earned their place on the countertop because they handle the jobs a full-size oven does poorly: quick reheats, crispy toast, and small-batch baking without heating up the whole kitchen. The key is matching size and features to your actual cooking habits, do you mostly toast bagels, or are you trying to air-fry wings for two? Look for even heating, a convection fan, and easy-clean interiors. Capacity matters: a model that fits a 12-inch pizza or a small chicken is more versatile, but it takes up more space. We sorted through owner feedback and expert reviews to find reliable models that actually deliver consistent results year after year.
